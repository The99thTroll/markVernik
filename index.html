From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the human being. For Marks in a parallel universe, see Mark Vernik (disambiguation).

A photo of Mark asserting dominance at Raynor Park

Mark Vernik, also known as mV on Discord, is an intelligent member of the Homo Sapien faction from Earth. As with all other Homo Sapiens, Mark consists of two arms, two legs, one torso, a head, and miraculously, an XXXL sized brain. He is most notably known for his involvement within the Apple Large Gang and has been a driving financial force behind the Official Peterson Applesauce Association (OPAA). Though that is what he is most known for, Mark has also been a part in several smaller, less profitable schemes and organizations.

Mark is infamously identified by his signature tank profile picture which as of recently has been replaced with mere landscape, much to fans' disappointment. However another signature trademark of Mark's is his bold, ignorance captious attitude. As a result of such a fearsome mentality, others have taken attempts to tame such a vigourous mood. Such attempts has failed however with the most famous example being "The Mark Clause" which was first created by Sticky Hands 'Brino and then adopted by Mr. Sandman.

Other Entrepreneurial Journeys

Mark Vernik while incredibly well known for his exploits within the OPAA, he has had other, less known and less successful operations. One of these was the "Company Yelp Review Anlalyzer" (okay I actually have no clue what the heck this one was called but lets roll with it). The idea behind this glorious idea that had come from Mark's massive brain was to analyze a company's google or yelp reviews and then point out the most important flaws/boons of the business. There was only one problem for Mark... Sticky Hands 'Brino. Unfortunately, due to 'Brino's less than passable work ethic, the project flopped due to a lack of updates. However, there are rumors that Mark has moved on and picked up the fragments of what he accomplished and is now working with Toyam Budiner in order to raise the project back from the dead and into the glory it had been promised.
Fragments of the project's planning can be found here, here, and here.

Moving past this failure, one would expect that Mark would move on and understand that Sticky Hands 'Brino is not to be trusted. As brilliant Mark is, I guess he's kinda dense... This time is was Sticky Hands 'Brino who had the idea. They would be selling World's Finest Chocolate to students in their school. For whatever reason, Mark decided he wanted to be an investor in this project. With many back and forth talks, discussions of royalties and whatnot, a deal was finally brokered. All this just for 'Brino not to follow through and drop the investment due to a lack of accessable funds and a lack of confidence on their side. Hopefully Mark sees the failures with 'Brino and will continue to outsource work for his further deals.

Rise of Anti-Mark Chats

There was another critical factor that led to the tragic downfall of Mark during wargamings: The Dreaded Anti-Mark Chats. While one may quickly assume it was his long standing rival Abe who organized his peers into a gang against him, it was actually Mark's good friend Sticky Hands 'Brino who was to blame! The reasoning behing this? "Just a spot of minor tomfoolery" said 'Brino [citation needed].

The aim of said chats was the prevent Mark from reaching success as much as possible. Now in the beginning this was an honorable goal as Mark was actually good at the game (he did not have a skill issue back then) but as time went on, the Anti-Mark Chats were far too overused to the point where people would specifically add Mark to these chats in order to derail the operation. With time, these chats faded in oblivion with not many still active. There are myths of one known as "Anti-Mark Chat B-Day Edition" which has been utilized recently but if that's true remains unclear.
